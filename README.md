in 2a, pink still has glimpse of hope. starts with hey you, "hey you, don't tell me there's no hope at all. together we stand, divided we fall." and ends with mental and physical breakdown in comfortably numb. unfortunately, in 2b, he is completely ill. worm, the judge, the mom, the wife is all his delusions.
